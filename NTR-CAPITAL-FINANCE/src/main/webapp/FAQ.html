Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to process a loan?

Loan processing time varies but typically takes between 24-72 hours after document submission, depending on the loan type and verification.

What is the eligibility to apply for a loan?

Eligibility depends on the loan type, but basic requirements are being an adult Indian resident with a reliable income source and the necessary documents submitted.

Is there a processing fee for loans?

Yes, a nominal processing fee may apply. The exact amount depends on the loan product. Please contact our team for current rates.

Can I foreclose or prepay my loan ahead of schedule?

Yes, foreclosure and prepayment are allowed on most loans, though charges or conditions may apply. Contact us for specific details.

How do I check the status of my loan application?

You can check your loan status by contacting our team via WhatsApp at 9156173284 or through your account/dashboard after logging in.

Which documents are mandatory for all loans?

Identity proof, address proof, income proof, and bank statements are generally mandatory. Some loans require special documents—see our ‘Required Documents’ section for full details.
